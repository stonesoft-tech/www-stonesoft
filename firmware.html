Firmware

We offer a full firmware development solution, to enable you to integrate microcontroller-based functionality to your electronic products. Starting with an initial assessment, we will assess various firmware platforms to determine the best solution for your needs. We can provide very basic implementations, to full RTOS driven multi-tasking designs.

Whatever your peripheral requirements, we can make your firmware work the way you want. We utilise manufacturer standard libraries, as well as building our own peripheral drivers where necessary. And with more and more products required cloud management, we can integrate your device with the Internet of Things (IoT).

We use SCRUM techniques as part of our development process, and once we have identified some user stories, we then build a full specification, and scope of works, which forms the basis of our sprints. At all times we use GIT for version control, allowing for parallel development, as well as ensuring roll backs if required.

If you need software apps to work with your devices, whether for the in-production test process, or for an enhanced user experience, we can provide that too.
